About

I am currently a machine learning engineer at AILabs.tw working on learning-based solutions to improve medical diagnosis procedures. Prior to joining the industry, I was lucky to have opportunities to work with Professor Min Sun at National Tsing Hua University and Dr. Le Lu from PAII Bethesda Research Lab. My research mainly focuses on computer vision and its applications in medical image computing. In particular, I am interested in vision for interaction, especially for the efficient computer-assisted medical interventions.
